FAQ

Common Questions About Our Lead Generation Service

How do you define a 'qualified lead'?
We define qualified leads as decision-makers or influencers at companies that match your ideal customer profile, who have expressed interest in your solution and agreed to a conversation. Each lead includes verified contact information and company details. We'll work with you to refine these criteria for your specific business needs.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients begin receiving qualified leads within the first 2-3 weeks after campaign launch. The first month focuses on establishing baselines and optimizing campaigns, with performance typically improving significantly in months 2-3 as we refine targeting and messaging based on initial results.
What industries do you specialize in?
We specialize in B2B lead generation across technology, professional services, manufacturing, healthcare, and financial services industries. Our team has specific experience with complex sales cycles and high-value offerings, making us particularly effective for B2B companies with average deal sizes above $10,000.
How is your service different from other lead generation companies?
Unlike most lead gen services that focus on volume over quality, we prioritize delivering leads that actually convert. Our multi-channel approach, rigorous qualification process, and continuous optimization ensure you receive consistent, high-quality leads. We also offer a 30-day performance guarantee that protects your investment.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract?
No. While we recommend a minimum 3-month commitment to see optimal results (as the first month involves setup and calibration), all our services operate on a month-to-month basis after the initial period. We believe in earning your business each month through performance.
